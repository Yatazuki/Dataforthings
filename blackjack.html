How to Play:

Try to get as close to 21 as possible without going over. Face cards are worth 10, Aces are 1 or 11.

  • Hit - Take another card
  • Stand - End your turn
  • Dealer must hit until they have at least 17
  • Blackjack (21 with first two cards) pays 1.5x your bet
